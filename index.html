About Us

La Forno began as a small family-run pizzeria with a simple mission: great ingredients, honest recipes, and a warm table. We bake our pizzas in a wood-fired oven and celebrate seasonal produce and timeless flavor combinations.

Accessibility and hospitality are important to us — our dining room is wheelchair accessible and our staff are trained to help with dietary questions.

Opening hours: Daily 11:00 — 23:00
